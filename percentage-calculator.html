What is a Percentage?

A percentage is a number expressed as a fraction of 100. It is used in exams, discounts, finance, and daily calculations.

How Percentage Calculator Works

Enter a percentage and a number, then click calculate. The tool instantly shows the result using standard percentage formula.

Formula

Percentage = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

Common Percentage Examples

  • 10% of 100 = 10
  • 20% of 500 = 100
  • 50% of 1000 = 500
  • 75% of 200 = 150

Where is Percentage Used?
