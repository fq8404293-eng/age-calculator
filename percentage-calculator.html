What is a Percentage?

A percentage is a way of expressing numbers as a fraction of 100. It is widely used in education, finance, shopping discounts, and business calculations worldwide including USA and India.

Who Uses Percentage Calculations?

Percentage calculations are used globally by students, teachers, business owners, shoppers, and financial analysts for everyday calculations like marks, discounts, and profit/loss.
