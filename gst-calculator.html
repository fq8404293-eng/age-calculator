What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a unified indirect tax applied on goods and services in India. It replaced multiple taxes like VAT, service tax, and excise duty.

How GST Calculator Works

Enter the amount, select GST rate, and click Add GST or Remove GST. The calculator instantly shows base price, GST amount, and total price.

GST Rates in India

  • 5% – Essential goods
  • 12% – Packaged food, mobile phones
  • 18% – Electronics, services
  • 28% – Luxury items
