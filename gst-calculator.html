What is GST in India?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a unified tax system used in India on the sale of goods and services. It replaced multiple indirect taxes like VAT, service tax, and excise duty.

Who Uses This GST Calculator?

This GST calculator is widely used in India by shop owners, freelancers, small businesses, accountants, and customers to quickly calculate tax on products and services.

GST Rates in India

  • 5% – Essential goods and services
  • 12% – Packaged food and telecom services
  • 18% – Most goods and services (standard rate)
  • 28% – Luxury items and premium goods
