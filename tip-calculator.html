What is a Tip?

A tip is an extra payment given to service staff for good service in restaurants, cafes, hotels, and delivery services. It is commonly used in countries like the USA, Canada, and many others.

Who Uses This Tip Calculator?

This tool is used by people in the USA, Canada, UK, and worldwide for splitting bills and calculating restaurant tips easily.

How Tip Calculator Works

Enter the bill amount, tip percentage, and number of people. The calculator instantly calculates the tip amount, total bill, and how much each person should pay when splitting the bill.

Formula

Tip = (Bill Amount × Tip %) ÷ 100
Total Bill = Bill Amount + Tip
Per Person = Total Bill ÷ Number of People
