What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement used to estimate whether your weight is healthy for your height. It is commonly used by health professionals worldwide.

How to Calculate BMI?

BMI is calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared.

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²)

BMI Calculator FAQs

What is a healthy BMI range for adults?

A healthy BMI range for adults is between 18.5 and 24.9 according to WHO guidelines used worldwide including USA and India.

Is BMI the same in all countries?

Yes, BMI calculation formula is the same globally, but interpretation may vary slightly by country health guidelines.

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a general indicator and may not be fully accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or people with high muscle mass.

What BMI is considered overweight?

A BMI between 25 and 29.9 is considered overweight in most global health standards.

What BMI indicates obesity?

A BMI of 30 or above is considered obese according to WHO classification.

Can BMI be used for children?

Yes, but children’s BMI is calculated using age and growth percentiles, not adult ranges.

Why is BMI used worldwide?

BMI is used because it is a simple, quick method to estimate body weight categories without expensive equipment.
