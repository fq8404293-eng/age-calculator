What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement used to estimate whether your weight is healthy for your height. It is commonly used by doctors and health professionals around the world.

How to Calculate BMI?

BMI is calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared.

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a healthy BMI?
A healthy BMI for most adults is between 18.5 and 24.9.

Is BMI accurate?
BMI is a useful screening tool, but it does not directly measure body fat percentage.
