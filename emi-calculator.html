What is an EMI Calculator?

An EMI Calculator helps you calculate your Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for loans. It shows your monthly EMI, total interest payable, and total payment amount.

How EMI is Calculated

EMI depends on three factors:

  • Loan Amount
  • Interest Rate
  • Loan Tenure

This EMI Calculator can be used for home loans, personal loans, education loans, and car loans.
