What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is a fixed monthly payment made by a borrower to repay a loan over time. It includes both principal and interest.

Who Uses This EMI Calculator?

EMI Calculator FAQs

What is EMI in simple words?

EMI is the fixed monthly payment you make to repay a loan including interest.

Is EMI same every month?

Yes, in most fixed-rate loans EMI remains the same throughout the loan tenure.

Can I use this EMI calculator for home loans?

Yes, it works for home loans, car loans, personal loans, and education loans.

Does interest rate affect EMI?

Yes, higher interest rate increases EMI and total repayment amount.
