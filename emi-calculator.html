What is an EMI Calculator?

An EMI Calculator helps you estimate the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for a loan. It shows your monthly EMI, total interest payable, and total repayment amount.

How EMI is Calculated

EMI depends on three main factors:

  • Loan Amount
  • Interest Rate
  • Loan Tenure

This calculator can be used for home loans, personal loans, education loans, and car loans.

Benefits of Using an EMI Calculator

  • Estimate monthly loan payments
  • Understand total interest costs
  • Compare different loan options
  • Plan your finances better
